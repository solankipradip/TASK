Atal Bridge

This iconic Atal bridge is first of its kind in the country. It will connect west and east part of Sabarmati Riverfront. It has drawn inspiration from the Kites and the celebration of Uttrayan. Even the colours chosen reflects the hues of kites. To feel the joy of walking above the river, this glass Atal bridge is constructed between Sardar Bridge and Ellis Bridge. The bridge is solely for the walking purpose and also having seating arrangements for the people to enjoy the beauty of the river from the bridge. Plantation of various trees are also done to have some green cover on the bridge. An Iconic Atal Bridge is constructed between Ellis Bridge and Sardar Bridge. It has eased and improved the pedestrian connectivity between the East and West side of the Riverfront. It has connected the core City in the East with the further developed city in the West and created an equilibrium and balancing in terms of City planning. It celebrates the importance of the Pedestrians. The Bridge is unique in its design â€“ both technically and visually. It is not just a pathway across the Sabarmati River but has become a destination in itself. Not only the People of Ahmedabad but People across the State and Country like to visit and spend time on the bridge amidst Riverine nature. This Iconic Bridge development has augmented the status of the Riverfront as well as of the Ahmedabad City and has become an Engineering marvel. The Bridge will provide connectivity to Multi Level Car Parking and various public developments on the East and West Bank from the plaza between Flower Park and Event Ground at West Bank to the proposed Art / Cultural / Exhibition Centre on the East Bank. The bridge is approachable from both Lower and Upper Promenade. The bridge has provided a picturesque view and has food kiosks (2 nos.), seating cum planters (14 nos.),transparent glass floor (4 nos. of 24 Sq.mt.), Kinetic Structure for Selfies and various other recreational facilities.
